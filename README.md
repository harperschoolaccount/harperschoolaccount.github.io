# Web-Ported Games

Welcome to our collection of three exciting web-ported games! Below you'll find a brief description of each game, instructions on how to play, and links to access them.

## 1. Schoolboy Runaway
**Description:**  
Schoolboy Runaway is an action-packed adventure game where players navigate through various obstacles while trying to escape from school. The game features vibrant graphics and engaging sound effects that enhance the gaming experience.

**How to Play:**  
- Use the arrow keys to move the character.
- Avoid obstacles and collect power-ups for extra points.
- Reach the finish line as fast as possible to earn a high score.

**Play it here:** [Schoolboy Runaway](http://example.com/schoolboy-runaway)

## 2. Ultra Kill
**Description:**  
Ultra Kill is a fast-paced shooter game that tests your reflexes and aim. Each level becomes progressively harder, filled with enemies trying to take you down.

**How to Play:**  
- Use WASD keys to move around the map.
- Click to shoot your weapon at enemies.
- Collect upgrades to enhance your firepower.

**Play it here:** [Ultra Kill](http://example.com/ultra-kill)

---

## 3. Buckshot Roulette
**Description:**  
Buckshot Roulette is a thrilling game of chance that combines elements of luck and strategy. Players must choose their bets wisely to win big!

**How to Play:**  
- Pick your bet amount before the round starts.
- Spin the wheel and hope for the best!
- Take risks for higher rewards but play it safe to keep your balance.

**Play it here:** [Buckshot Roulette](http://example.com/buckshot-roulette)
